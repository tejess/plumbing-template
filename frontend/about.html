About Apex Plumbing Co.

Family-owned and operated since 1993. Serving New York City with honesty, skill, and pride.

Our Story

Built on 30 Years of Hard Work

Apex Plumbing Co. was founded in 1993 by Frank Rossi, who spent his early career as an apprentice under some of Brooklyn's best master plumbers. After earning his New York State Master Plumber License in 1991, Frank spent two years working for larger firms before deciding to go out on his own.

He started with one truck, one toolbox, and a commitment to never leave a job site until the customer was 100% satisfied. Word spread quickly through Bay Ridge and Bensonhurst, and within three years, Frank had hired his first two employees.

Today, Apex Plumbing is a team of eight licensed plumbers serving all five boroughs of New York City. We've completed over 5,000 jobs — from a dripping kitchen faucet in a studio apartment to a full commercial pipe replacement in a 20-story Midtown building.

We've never taken on investors, never franchised, and never sent a subcontractor to a job without our direct supervision. Every plumber on our team is personally hired and trained by Frank, holds a valid New York City plumber's license, and has passed a comprehensive background check.
